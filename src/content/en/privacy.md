## Privacy Policy

10dos is a minimal browser extension. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

### Data storage

- Your tasks are stored **only on your device** in the browser’s local storage.
- No data is sent to our servers or any third party.
- Uninstalling the extension removes this local data.

### No account, no tracking

- 10dos does not require sign-up or an account.
- We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking on the extension or this website beyond what is needed to serve the site.

### Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version is always on this page.

If you have questions, you can reach us via the contact information on the main site.
